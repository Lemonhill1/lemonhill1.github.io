Welcome to Blood Banks

This band rips. Blood Banks is a rock n’ roll four-piece from Minneapolis, MN. They formed in 2015 and have released two EP’s. Their songs tear away pretense, remember friends gone too soon and reflect on broken dreams. At its core, Blood Banks music celebrates survival.
- Dan Russell (Record Industry Mogul)

News

We are very pleased to announce that our brand new record, ‘Dear Tomorrow’ is now available in digital form for your listening pleasure. It can currently be downloaded HERE, or streamed via SoundCloud, YouTube. iTunes, Spotify, and other streaming services should have it available soon as well.
