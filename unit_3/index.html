Welcome to Blood Banks

This band rips. Blood Banks is a rock n’ roll four-piece from Minneapolis, MN. They formed in 2015 and have released two EP’s. Their songs tear away pretense, remember friends gone too soon and reflect on broken dreams. At its core, Blood Banks music celebrates survival. Dan Russell (Record Industry Mogul)

News

We are very pleased to announce that our brand new record, "Dear Tomorrow" is now available in digital form for your listening pleasure. It can currently be downloaded HERE, or streamed via SoundCloud, YouTube. iTunes, Spotify, and other streaming services should have it available soon as well.

Many thanks to Jeremy Tappero at Little Red Light and Matt Allison at Atlas for the engineering, mixing and mastering. Thanks to Lucy Glatt for lending us her voice, and James Russell for lending us his guitar for the sessions as well. Lastly, thanks to all of our friends for listening, raising a fist and singing along.
