THIS IS BLOOD BANKS

This band rips. Blood Banks is a rock n’ roll four-piece from Minneapolis, MN. They formed in 2015 and have released two EP’s. Their songs tear away pretense, remember friends gone too soon and reflect on broken dreams. At its core, Blood Banks music celebrates survival. Blood Banks songs stand in the face of the past while moving towards a future that so many of their beloved never saw. Lessons learned and gratitude; living for tomorrow through the lens of yesterday, and trying to make sense of it all. These are the themes of Blood Banks music.
Dan Russell (Record Industry Mogul)
